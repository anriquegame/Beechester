Places of Interest in Manchester

Discover some of Manchester's most captivating spots, from lush parks and historic landmarks to vibrant neighborhoods and hidden gems. Explore each place with our interactive map and photo gallery below.

Heaton Park

One of the largest municipal parks in Europe, Heaton Park features beautiful landscapes, an animal centre, tram tours, and a historic hall.

Northern Quarter

Known for its creative vibe, street art, independent shops, and buzzing nightlife. Perfect for urban exploration and local culture.

Chinatown

Food, festivals, and lanterns bring Manchester’s vibrant Chinatown to life, offering authentic cuisines and cultural celebrations.

Albert Square

Home to Victorian architecture and buzzing events, Albert Square hosts markets, concerts, and the famous Christmas tree each December.

The Lowry

Located in Salford Quays, The Lowry showcases contemporary art, theatre performances, and galleries overlooking the waterfront.

Old Trafford

Old Trafford are home to football legends — catch matches or take stadium tours immersed in sporting history.

Parks in Manchester

Wander through Manchester’s diverse parks, ideal for picnics and family days out.

Hotels

Discover a range of accommodations near Manchester’s must-see sights, from charming boutique B&Bs in the city centre to upscale hotels in Salford Quays.
