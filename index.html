Welcome to Beechester, your quick guide to Manchester.

Once called “Cottonopolis” as the world's first industrial city powered by 18th century mills and canals, Manchester now buzzes with culture, football and innovation. From top matches at Old Trafford (Manchester United) and the Etihad (Manchester City) to world class museums, live music venues and its UNESCO City of Literature status, there's something for every explorer.
