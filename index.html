Manchester's City Centre is a mix of history, style and personality. From the shops of Market Street to the indie vibe of the Northern Quarter, there's always something to see, eat or experience. Grab a coffee, explore local art, or just enjoy the buzz. Whether you're new in town or a local out for the day, the centre never feels the same twice.
