Practical Information

Your guide to key facilities, transport, accommodation, and airport details in Manchester.

Sporting Facilities, Buildings of Distinction & Landmarks

Old Trafford

Home of Manchester United FC, seating 74,310. Stadium tours available daily. See museum and trophy room.

Manchester Aquatics Centre

Olympic-standard pool complex at the University of Manchester, featuring 50m pools and diving boards.

Manchester Town Hall

Iconic Gothic Revival building at Albert Square. Free guided architecture tours on weekends.

Manchester Cathedral

Historic medieval cathedral with striking nave and stained glass. Open daily for visitors.

Public Transport & Airport Information

  • Metrolink: Light rail network connecting city centre to suburbs, Salford Quays, and direct airport tram. Purchase tickets via app or machines.
  • Buses: Extensive bus services across Greater Manchester. Use the Bee Network Go app for real-time schedules.
  • Trains: Stations at Piccadilly, Victoria, Oxford Road link Manchester to UK cities; Airport train to Piccadilly (~20 mins).
  • Coaches: National Express depart from Chorlton Street Coach Station; airport coaches outside terminals.
  • Taxi: Licensed ranks at all airport terminals; journey to city centre ~30 mins.
