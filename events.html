Upcoming Cultural Events & Festivals

Discover the very best of Beechester's vibrant cultural scene. From music and art to family-friendly festivals and seasonal markets, find out what's on around town and plan your next adventure.

Manchester International Festival

Date: 3 July – 20 July

Venue: Various Venues across Manchester

The world’s first festival of original work, showcasing unique performances in theatre, music, art and more.

Parklife Festival

Date: 14 June – 15 June

Venue: Heaton Park

A weekend of live music and street food, featuring top DJs and bands in a beautiful park setting.

Manchester Jazz Festival

Date: 16 May – 25 May

Venue: Various Venues including Band on the Wall & RNCM

A celebration of jazz in all its forms, featuring local and international talent across intimate clubs and grand halls.

Manchester Literature Festival

Date: 10 October – 26 October

Venue: Various Libraries & Theatres

A week-long celebration of words with readings, workshops, and author Q&A sessions.

Manchester Christmas Markets

Date: 8 November – 22 December

Venue: Albert Square, St Ann’s Square & King Street

Experience Europe’s largest Christmas market with festive stalls, mulled wine, artisan crafts, and seasonal treats.
